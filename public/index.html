The Tech Opportunity Fund is the product of a public-private partnership of organizations committed to providing access to technology education for all. The Fund is designed to remove financial barriers for people who are underrepresented in the technology industry, including but not limited to: women, people of color, military personnel and people with disabilities. Students with financial need are encouraged to apply to receive a full-tuition scholarship to attend a full-time, immersive code school and take the first step toward a fulfilling career in technology.

Applications will open Fall 2016.

The Commitment:

Education Partners

Through accelerated, results-driven education, code schools are an effective model for creating pathways to quality, well-paying tech jobs. The Tech Opportunity Fund’s Education Partners are committing to providing access to tech education by reserving spots in their classes for those underrepresented in the tech industry and who demonstrate financial need.

The Commitment:

Employment Partners

Employment Partners can support the Tech Opportunity Fund by being open to hiring candidates from accelerated tech training programs. Companies can participate in a variety of ways, from contributing financially to the fund, supporting the in-class experience and, most importantly, building hiring and apprenticeship programs for scholarship recipients.

Interested in becoming an Employment Partner?

The Commitment:

Civic Partners

Civic Partners like nonprofits, workforce development agencies and government bodies will work with the Tech Opportunity Fund to meet the full needs of students. These partners are committing to taking measurable actions to support scholarship recipients including providing financial aid, subsidizing short-term housing, assisting with access to transportation and providing free counseling. The White House has endorsed this program and is actively encouraging as many cities as possible to participate.

Interested in becoming a Civic Partner?

The Commitment:

Accountability

The Tech Opportunity Fund is committed to being transparent and accountable to its students and partners. The outcomes of the scholarship program will be audited by a third-party to ensure that the Fund is always working toward the best interest of the students.
